## The Problem: The Sneaker Market Moves Fast
If you run a sneaker resale business on Shopify, you know that prices for a Jordan 1 or a Yeezy Slide can swing $20-$50 in a single day. 

*   **Manual Updates:** Spending hours every morning checking StockX is exhausting.
*   **Lost Profits:** Selling too low because you didn't notice a market jump.
*   **Zero Sales:** Pricing too high because you didn't see the market dip.

**SyncKicks** solves this by bridging the gap between StockX market data and your Shopify inventory.

---

## Key Features

### Daily Automatic Price Syncing
Your store stays updated while you sleep. The app pulls the latest "Last Sale" or "Lowest Ask" data from StockX and updates your Shopify product variants automatically.

### Custom Pricing Rules
You don't always want to match StockX exactly. Our flexible engine lets you set rules:
*   **Match:** Mirror StockX prices 1:1.
*   **Percentage Based:** Set your prices to be +5%, -2% or any percentage you want, relative to the market.
*   **Flat Rate:** Stay exactly $15 or any fixed amount you want, above StockX to cover your overhead.

### Margin Protection (Floor & Ceiling)
This is our most important feature. You retain total control.
*   **Price Floor:** Set a minimum price for every SKU. The app will *never* drop your price below this number, ensuring you never sell for a loss.
*   **Price Ceiling:** Prevent your prices from spiking too high and looking like "price gouging" during a hype bubble.
